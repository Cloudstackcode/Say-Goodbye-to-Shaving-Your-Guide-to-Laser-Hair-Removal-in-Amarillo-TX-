What to Expect When You Visit Us for Laser Hair Removal

We always start with a consultation. During your first visit, we’ll sit down with you to discuss your hair removal goals and assess your skin type, hair color, and any underlying health conditions. This isn’t just a formality—it’s a critical part of ensuring your safety and the success of your treatments.

Laser hair removal works by targeting the pigment in hair follicles with a concentrated beam of light. This light energy damages the follicle, slowing or stopping future growth. Because hair grows in cycles, multiple sessions are necessary to target each follicle during its active growth phase.

Most of our clients require six to eight treatments for best results, spaced a few weeks apart. We’ll create a custom schedule based on your skin and hair characteristics. During the procedure, you might feel a slight snapping sensation, similar to a rubber band against the skin, but most clients report it’s more comfortable than waxing.

Our medical professionals use FDA-approved equipment and follow strict protocols for hygiene and safety. You can relax knowing you’re in skilled hands.

Tips for Getting the Best Results

If you’re thinking about laser hair removal, here are a few tips to help you prepare:

  • Avoid sun exposure for two weeks before treatment. Tanned skin can interfere with laser accuracy.
  • Shave the area 24 hours before your appointment. This allows the laser to target the follicle directly.
  • Avoid waxing or plucking for a few weeks prior. The follicle needs to be intact for the treatment to be effective.
  • Follow aftercare instructions carefully. This includes avoiding hot baths, tanning beds, and certain skincare products for a few days.

Following these steps will help ensure your treatments are as effective and comfortable as possible.
